Why honesty is important?

Let's follow the policy- honesty guys! Swikar believes that the word HONESTY is not only just a word but a magic in itself. He wants us to know that the act and quality of being honest is essential in human life. He further explains the importance of honesty with the saying of Benjamin franklin. He is highly inspired with the saying “Honesty is the best policy” He interestingly finds the best solution to overcome the present pandemic situation through HONESTY. He shares the beautiful secret that HONESTY is the cause of success. As a team is the heart of every development, honesty is the heart of a team. He beautifully elaborates on why honesty is essential to every student and why students need to adopt this quality for overall personal and academic development. He insists us to follow HONESTY in life if we are willing to be contained with qualitative progresses.
